==============================================================================
  Spider-Man: Kraven ka Aakhri Shikaar
  LOG LINE: Kraven the Hunter ek boodha aur fakhri shikaari hai. Apni maut ka ehsaas hote hi woh Spider-Man ko ek potent tranquilizer goli se ghol kar zinda qabar kar deta hai aur khud Spider-Man ka kapda pehen leta hai. Do hafte baad Peter qabar se bahar nikalta hai. Ek naya Spider-Man sheher mein brutal ho chuka hai. Ab dono ka aakhri muqabla.
==============================================================================

CHARACTERS
----------------------------------------
  SPIDER-MAN (PETER PARKER)      Protagonist
  KRAVEN THE HUNTER              Antagonist
  MARY JANE WATSON-PARKER        Emotional Anchor
  VERMIN (BILLY RUSSKOFF)        Monster / Minion


  EXT. NYC STREETS AT NIGHT - DARK RAINY ALLEY - 

  Dark rainy night in NYC. Kraven the Hunter stalks through wet streets
  with a high-powered rifle. He spots Spider-Man swinging between
  buildings. Kraven aims carefully and fires a potent tranquilizer
  bullet. Spider-Man is hit in the chest and collapses on the rooftop.
  Kraven drags the unconscious Spider-Man to a cemetery and buries him
  alive under pouring rain. Moonlight shines on fresh grave.
  Kraven: Main ne duniya ke sabse khatarnaak janwar shikaar kiye hain. Lekin aaj sabse khatarnaak shikaar karne wala hoon. Spider-Man. Main uski jagah lunga.

  EXT. KRAVENS APARTMENT - MIRROR SCENE - 

  Kraven stands in front of a large mirror wearing the Spider-Man
  costume. He practices web-swinging movements awkwardly then more
  confidently. He shoots a web line at a wall and tests it. His facial
  expression in the mask is intense and feral not heroic. He smiles
  cruelly at his reflection. Cut to him walking through NYC streets
  where people gasp at this new brutal Spider-Man who beats criminals
  instead of webbing them.
  Kraven: Yeh kapda pehen kar main Spider-Man se behtar ban jaunga. Yeh sheher mujhe pehchanega. Main woh karunga jo woh kabhi nahi karta.

  EXT. CEMETERY - UNDERGROUND GRAVE - 

  Complete darkness underground. Peter Parker digs frantically with bare
  hands bleeding profusely. He gasps for precious air. Visions appear
  around him - a giant spider, feral beast eyes, shadows of Vermin. The
  spider voice whispers to become a beast. Peter resists, clutches a
  photo of Mary Jane to his chest, and pushes upward with all his
  remaining strength. Earth gives way - he bursts through the surface
  into moonlight, collapsing on fresh grass, gasping and alive but
  broken.
  Peter: Mary Jane mujhe wapas jaana hai main insaan hoon janwar nahi.

  EXT. MJS APARTMENT - REUNION - 

  Night. Mary Jane opens the apartment door. Peter stands there bruised
  bleeding barely standing. MJ screams in terror then catches him and
  brings him inside. She tends to his wounds with trembling hands. Peter
  tries to get up insisting he must stop Kraven. MJ holds him back
  crying desperately. Peter pulls away gently and forces himself into
  the Spider-Man suit, weak but resolute.
  MJ: Peter mat jaao tum mar jaoge! Peter: Main do hoon MJ. Dono. Aur aaj apni zimmedari nibhaunga.

  EXT. ABANDONED WAREHOUSE - THE FIGHT - 

  An abandoned warehouse dimly lit. Spider-Man enters cautiously. Kraven
  is waiting sitting calmly on a wooden crate with no weapons. Spider-
  Man attacks furiously. Kraven dodges weakly barely defends refusing to
  fight back. Kraven releases the door to a heavy steel cage - Vermin
  bursts out feral and monstrous. Weakened Spider-Man fights but gets
  brutally beaten. Vermin pins him down. Kraven watches calmly
  satisfied. Spider-Man barely escapes by firing webs to pin Vermin to
  the wall. Kraven walks away his hunt complete.
  Kraven: Dekha? Tum haar gaye. Main Spider-Man se behtar hoon. Spider-Man: Yeh khatam nahi hua. Main hoon aur hamesha rahunga.

  EXT. NYC ROOFTOP AT DAWN - 

  Dawn breaks over NYC skyline. Kraven sits alone on rooftop Spider-Man
  costume removed wearing hunting clothes holding the rifle. Flashbacks:
  young Kraven hunting deer, first encounter with Spider-Man, the night
  he buried him alive, beating criminals as Spider-Man. He looks
  peaceful. He writes a detailed confession letter with photographic
  proof. He loads one bullet into the rifle, looks at the beautiful
  rising sun, and pulls the trigger. His body slumps gently. The
  confession letter and photos are beside him.
  Kraven: Main ne poora jeevan shikaar kiya. Aakhri shikaar mera khud ka tha. Spider-Man tum jeet gaye. Lekin mujhe shaanti mil gayi.

  EXT. CITY STREETS - VERMIN CHASE AND AFTERMATH - 

  Spider-Man chases Vermin through dark alleyways. Vermin is fast and
  feral running on all fours. Spider-Man is weak but uses intelligence,
  luring Vermin toward the surface through manholes. They break through
  into a bright noisy city intersection with traffic and crowds. Vermins
  senses overload - bright sunlight, car horns, and city noise render
  him helpless. He collapses screaming. Spider-Man webs him to a
  lamppost exhausted but victorious. Police arrive. Spider-Man looks at
  sunrise then swings away into the city skyline.
  Spider-Man: Isse reed richards ko ilaaj milega. Yeh janwar nahi ek insaan hai jo bimar hai.

  FADE OUT.
